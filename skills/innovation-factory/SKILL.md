# Innovation Factory

Transform research synthesis into actionable innovation opportunities. Brainstorm ideas, evaluate feasibility, and rank opportunities.

## When to Use

- Have research synthesis output
- Need to generate innovation ideas
- Want to evaluate ideas by feasibility and impact

## Core Workflow

1. **Input from Research** - Load themes, gaps, opportunities
2. **Ideation Phase** - Generate multiple ideas per opportunity
3. **Idea Refinement** - Group similar ideas
4. **Evaluation Matrix** - Rate feasibility, impact, novelty
5. **Ranking** - Select top opportunities

## Evaluation Dimensions

- **Feasibility**: Technical, resource, regulatory, market
- **Impact**: User benefit, market size, strategic value
- **Novelty**: Innovation level, disruption potential

## Output Format

```markdown
# Innovation Opportunities: [Topic]

## Top Opportunities (Ranked)

### 1. [Innovation Idea]
- Pitch: [1-2 sentence]
- Feasibility: [Score 1-10]
- Impact: [Score 1-10]
- Total Score: [Score]

## Recommendations
1. [Recommended next steps]
```
